# Jurisdiction Source Map

Verified: April 10, 2026

This file captures the first concrete source surfaces for Marin v1.

The goal is not exhaustive coverage yet. The goal is to pin the highest-value official surfaces for:

- meetings
- agenda materials
- appointments
- campaign money
- disclosures
- contracts

## Marin County

### Board of Supervisors

- Official meetings page: https://www.marincounty.gov/departments/board/board-supervisors-meetings
- What it provides:
  agendas, reports, minutes, videos, participation instructions, archive access
- Notes:
  the page says meeting materials are available from 2005 to present, with a 1995-2005 archive and pre-1995 records available in person

### Clerk of the Board

- Official page: https://www.marincounty.gov/departments/board/clerk-board
- What it provides:
  administrative context for the board record system and official-record stewardship

### Boards, Commissions, and Public Hearings

- Official page: https://www.marincounty.gov/departments/cda/planning/boards-commissions-and-public-hearings
- What it provides:
  county board and commission meeting links, hearing links, staff reports, minutes, and videos for planning-related bodies

### Planning Commission

- Official page: https://www.marincounty.gov/departments/cda/planning/boards-commissions-and-public-hearings/about-planning-commission
- What it provides:
  commission mandate, appointment context, and appeal path to the Board of Supervisors

### Parks and Open Space Commission

- Official page: https://parks.marincounty.gov/about-us/boards-and-commissions/pos-commission
- What it provides:
  commission purpose plus meeting calendar and archive entry point

### Campaign Finance

- Marin County candidate guide: https://www.marincounty.gov/departments/elections/current-election/candidate-guide/campaign-finance-reporting-and-information
- NetFile portal: https://netfile.com/agency/cmar/
- What it provides:
  local campaign e-filing guidance plus the public access portal for campaign contributions, expenditures, and committee filing history

### Public Agency Registry

- Registry page: https://arcc.marincounty.gov/general-information/about-our-department/marin-county-clerks-office/registry-public-agencies
- What it provides:
  a route into local public agencies and special districts

### Priority Gaps

- county contracts and vendor payments
- county grants to nonprofits and service providers
- county advisory bodies outside planning and parks
- county-side Form 700 / Form 803 access surfaces

## San Rafael

### City Council Meetings

- Official page: https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/city-council-meetings/
- What it provides:
  agenda, packet, minutes, and video links for city council meetings

### Public Meetings Hub

- Official page: https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/departments/public-meetings/
- Supporting guide: https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/guide-public-meetings/
- What it provides:
  meeting participation instructions plus a city-level entry point into public meetings

### Boards, Commissions, and Committees

- Official page: https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/boards-commissions/
- Meetings index: https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/board-and-commission-meetings/
- What it provides:
  body directory, vacancy context, appointment context, and links to meeting archives

### Planning Commission

- Official page: https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/planning-commission/
- What it provides:
  meeting cadence, bylaws, member list, and commission purpose

### Police Advisory and Accountability Committee

- Official meetings page: https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/paac-meetings/
- What it provides:
  agenda, packet, minutes, and video archive for the police accountability body

### Disclosures

- Official page: https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/disclosures/
- What it provides:
  campaign filings context, conflict-of-interest materials, Form 700 guidance, Form 804, Form 806, and independent expenditure ordinance reference

### City Clerk

- Official page: https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/departments/city-clerks-office/
- What it provides:
  records-management context, elections guidance, and the city office that manages council records, ordinances, resolutions, minutes, and contracts

### Procurement / Contract Leads

- Bids and proposals: https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/bids-and-proposals/
- What it provides:
  active procurement opportunities and a foothold for contract-surface discovery

### Priority Gaps

- historical contract awards in structured form
- campaign filing portal if separate from city disclosures workflow
- roster of city officials in a stable machine-readable form
- subcommittee archives
- planning project document index across eTRAKiT and meeting records

## Working Notes

- San Rafael appears to have unusually strong public meeting pages with separate archive pages for many boards and commissions. This is high leverage for v1.
- Marin County’s board page is also strong and explicitly includes reports, minutes, and videos.
- Both jurisdictions appear suitable for a first implementation focused on one county-level and one city-level process graph.
