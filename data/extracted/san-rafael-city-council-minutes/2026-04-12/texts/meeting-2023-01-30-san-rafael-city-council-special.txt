Mayor Kate called the meeting to order at 4:30 p.m. and requested City Clerk Lindsay Lara call the roll. All
members of the City Council were present. Mayor Kate announced that the public comment would be
held at the end of the Study Session.

City Clerk Lindsay Lara informed the community that the in-person meeting would also be recorded
and streamed live through Zoom, and members of the public would provide public comment either on
the telephone or through Zoom. She explained the process for community participation on the
telephone, through Zoom and in-person.
