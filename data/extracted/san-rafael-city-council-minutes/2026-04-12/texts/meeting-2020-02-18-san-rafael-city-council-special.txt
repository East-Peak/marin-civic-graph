Special Meeting
San Rafael City Council                    Minutes

Present:      Mayor Pro Tem Colin
              Councilmember Bushey
              Councilmember McCullough

Absent:       Mayor Phillips
              Councilmember Gamblin

Also          City Manager Jim Schutz
Present:      City Clerk Lindsay Lara
              Library and Recreation Director Susan Andrade-Wax

Mayor Pro Tem Colin called the meeting to order at 5:34 p.m.

1.    Pickleweed Advisory Committee Interviews Interview Applicants and Consider
      Appointments to Fill Two Unexpired Four-Year Terms, One Regular Voting
      Member and One Alternate Member, on the Pickleweed Advisory Committee to the
      End of December 2022 (CC)
      Pickleweed Advisory Committee Interviews

       The City Council interviewed the following applicants: Keyly Gonzalez, Janet Tanchez
       and Jairo Ceballo. Andrew Cullen did not attend the interview.

       After discussion, there was City Council consensus to appoint Janet Tanchez to the
       voting member term, and appoint Jairo Ceballos as an Alternate member, on the
       Pickleweed Advisory Committee to the end of December 2022.

ADJOURNMENT:
Mayor Pro Tem Colin adjourned the meeting at 6:01 p.m.
