PUBLIC HEARINGS:
6.   Public Hearings:

a.   General Plan 2040 / Downtown Precise Plan
     General Plan 2040 Land Use Map - Preliminary Review and Discussion of
     Proposed Map Changes (CD)
     General Plan 2040 / Downtown Precise Plan

      Community Development Director Paul Jensen commented on the item and introduced
      Barry Miller who presented the staff report

      Staff responded to questions from the City Council

      Mayor Pro Tem Colin declared the public hearing opened

      Speakers: Bill Carney, Sustainable San Rafael, Brad Sears, Cathy Manovi

      There being no further comment from the audience, Mayor Pro Tem Colin declared the
      public hearing closed

      Staff responded to questions from the public

      Staff responded to questions from the City Council.

      The City Council provided feedback to staff and recommended the Sea Level Rise
      Adaptation Overlay Zone be re-titled and that references to the boundary being based
      on a “prediction” be clarified. The City Council also commented on the Downtown build-
       out data and recommended consistent mapping protocol be used for the designation of
       private schools.

       Councilmember McCullough moved and Councilmember Bushey seconded to accept
       the report and provided feedback to staff

       Accepted report and provided feedback to staff

       AYES:         Councilmembers: Bushey, McCullough & Mayor Pro Tem Colin
