Mayor Kate called the meeting to order at 6:03 p.m. and invited City Clerk Lindsay Lara to call the
roll. All members of the City Council were present, except for Councilmember Kertz.

City Attorney Robert Epstein announced that no reportable action was taken in Closed Session for
item 2.b, and the City Council would adjourn to Closed Session to continue the meeting for item
2.a.

Mayor Kate provided opening remarks which included gratitude to City Staff, announcing the
Employee of the Year Hector Carlos, Code Enforcement Supervisor and the Team of the Year, San
Rafael Field Training Officer Team, Sergeant Robert Cleland, Sergeant Justin Graham, Sergeant
Scott Ingels, Corporal Rick Cuddy, Corporal Scott Brooks, Corporal Dennis Prince, Corporal Justin
Schraeder, Officer Joe Jordan, Officer Brian Patterson, Officer Chris Heinlein and Officer Jennifer
Gamble, and a land acknowledgment.

City Clerk Lindsay Lara informed the community that the in-person meeting would also be recorded
and streamed live to YouTube and through Zoom. She noted the two-minute timer for public
comment and closed captioning on Zoom.
