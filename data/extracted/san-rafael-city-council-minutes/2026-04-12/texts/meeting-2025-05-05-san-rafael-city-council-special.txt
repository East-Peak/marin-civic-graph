Mayor Kate called the meeting to order at 4:00 p.m. and invited City Clerk Lindsay Lara to call the roll.
All members of the City Council were present, except Councilmember Kertz who was absent.

    a. City Council Goals and Objectives
       The City Council will conduct a goals and objectives workshop. The purpose of the
       workshop is to build alignment around the City Council’s vision for the next three-year
       goals and objectives plan period. The City Council will review and discuss draft goals and
       objectives which will inform the City’s budget development process and provide a
       roadmap for organizational success.

        Assistant City Manager John Stefanski provided background on the draft Goals and Objectives,
        the recommended changes from the previous study session and the reviewed the process for
        the evening.

        Mayor Kate invited public comment.

        Speakers: Bill Carney Sustainable San Rafael, Amy Likover, Victoria DeWitt, Yavar Amidi of
        Raíces Del Canal, Marley Brezzo
