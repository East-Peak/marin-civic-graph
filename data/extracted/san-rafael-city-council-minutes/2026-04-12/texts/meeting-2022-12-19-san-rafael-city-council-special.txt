Mayor Kate called the meeting to order at 6:32 p.m. and requested Deputy City Clerk Brenna Nurmi call
the roll. All members of the City Council were present.

Deputy City Clerk Brenna Nurmi informed the community that the in-person meeting would also be
recorded and streamed live to YouTube and through Zoom, and members of the public would
provide public comment either on the telephone or through Zoom. She explained the process for
community participation on the telephone, through Zoom and in-person.
