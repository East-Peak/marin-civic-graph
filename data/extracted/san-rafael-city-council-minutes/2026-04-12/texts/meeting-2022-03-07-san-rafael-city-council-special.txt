      Interview Applicants and Make an Appointment to Fill One Four-Year Term and One
      Alternate Member Four-Year Term to the End of March 2026 on the Fire Commission Due to

      end of March 2026 and Donna McCusker to fill one alternate member four-year term to the
      end of March 2026.
