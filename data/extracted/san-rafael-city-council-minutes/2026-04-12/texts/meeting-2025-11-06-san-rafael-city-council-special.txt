SPECIAL MEETING

    a. Listening Session and Informational Meeting Regarding the Potential Purchase of 350
       Merrydale Road for Interim Shelter and Affordable Housing Projects
       The purpose of this meeting is to provide information and receive community input regarding
       the City’s potential purchase of 350 Merrydale Road for future interim shelter and affordable
       housing projects. Members of the public are invited to share their comments directly with the
       City Council. This meeting is intended as a listening session; no decisions will be made and no
       formal action will be taken.

        In compliance with the Brown Act, City Councilmembers will not engage in dialogue or respond
        to questions during the meeting. However, staff will be available to provide factual information
        and respond to general questions about the project. Additionally, community members may
        submit comments by email to city.clerk@cityofsanrafael.org.

        City Attorney Robert Epstein invited public comment.
